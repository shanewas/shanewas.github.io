About Me

I'm a senior software engineer at SkyCom Corporation in Miyazaki, Japan, building enterprise software across C++/C# libraries, REST APIs, Linux deployment pipelines, and digital signature-related systems used by organizations nationwide.

Over 10 years across Bangladesh and Japan, I've shipped production systems at scale, co-founded a startup, led teams, and helped build automation software deployed to major financial institutions. I also contributed to a patent publication related to an electronic signature system using Japan's My Number Card.

I also co-authored an IEEE-published deep learning paper on Alzheimer's disease classification — joint research with the University of Sydney — bridging my interest in machine learning with real-world research.
